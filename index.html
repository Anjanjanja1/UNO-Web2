Help Information

Here you can find instructions about how to play UNO.

  • Each player is dealt 7 cards to start the game.
  • Match the card in the discard pile by color or number to play your card.
  • Special cards like Reverse, Draw Two, and Skip can make the game more exciting!
  • Don't forget to say "UNO" when you have only one card left!
