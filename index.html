UNO - Quick Start Guide
Objective

Be the first to play all your cards.

Win by being the first to reach 500 points after multiple rounds.

Setup
  • Deal 7 cards to each player.
  • Remaining cards form the DRAW PILE.
  • Turn over the top card to start the DISCARD PILE.
  • Play starts clockwise from the first player.
Gameplay

Match the DISCARD PILE card by color, number, or symbol.

Card Types & Actions
  • Number Cards: Match by color or number.
  • Skip: Next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse: Reverses play direction.
  • Draw Two: Next player draws 2 cards and skips their turn.
  • Wild: Choose the next color.
  • Wild+4: Choose the next color and next player draws 4 cards.
Scoring

Points are awarded based on the cards left in opponents' hands:

  • Number Cards: Face value
  • Draw Two, Reverse, Skip: 20 points each
  • Wild+4: 50 points each

First to reach 500 points wins.
